URBAN Sketchers

Urban Sketchers collaboration brings artists together to capture cityscapes, architecture, and daily life through spontaneous sketches. It fosters creativity, community, and a shared passion for documenting the world through art.

InkTober'24

Inktober'24 is a global creative challenge where artists create daily ink drawings throughout October. It encourages creativity, skill-building, and community engagement, with participants sharing their unique interpretations of themed prompts.
